This document outlines the preliminary findings from our user research study conducted during Q1. The study involved 45 participants across three geographic regions and focused on understanding user preferences for the new dashboard interface.

Key findings indicate a strong preference for customizable widgets (78% of participants) and a simplified navigation structure (65%). The data suggests that our current prototype aligns well with user expectations, though several areas for improvement were identified.

Next steps include incorporating feedback into the design iteration cycle and scheduling follow-up sessions with a subset of participants for usability testing of the revised prototype.
