Additional Notes

After completing the 5-step workflow, your project will be created and you'll be redirected to the project dashboard. From there, you can invite additional team members, configure integrations, and start adding tasks.

If you encounter any issues during project creation, consult the Troubleshooting Guide or contact support via the Help button in the bottom-right corner of the platform.
