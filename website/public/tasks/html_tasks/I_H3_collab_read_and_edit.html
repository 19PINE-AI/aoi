Research Summary

Prepared by the Analytics Team, April 2026

Summary

Our analysis of user engagement data from Q1 2026 reveals a 23% increase in daily active users compared to the previous quarter. Mobile usage now accounts for 67% of all sessions, up from 54%. The most significant growth was observed in the 25-34 age demographic, which grew by 31%. Retention rates improved across all cohorts, with 30-day retention reaching 42%, the highest level since product launch.

Methodology

Data was collected from our analytics platform covering January 1 through March 31, 2026. We applied standard cohort analysis techniques and controlled for seasonal variations using year-over-year comparisons.

Daily active users: 145,000 (avg). Monthly active users: 380,000. Session duration: 8.2 minutes (avg). Pages per session: 4.7.
