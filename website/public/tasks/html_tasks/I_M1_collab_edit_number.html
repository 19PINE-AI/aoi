Budget Overview — FY2026

Section 1: Revenue Projections

Based on current trends, we project total revenue of $2.4 million for FY2026. This represents a 15% increase over the previous fiscal year. The primary growth driver is the expansion into enterprise clients, which now account for 62% of recurring revenue.

Section 2: Operating Expenses

Total operating expenses for Q1 were $380 thousand. This includes personnel costs, infrastructure, marketing spend, and administrative overhead. We expect this figure to stabilize in subsequent quarters as hiring ramp-up completes.

Section 3: Capital Allocation

The board approved a capital expenditure budget of $150 thousand for new equipment and software licenses. An additional $75 thousand has been earmarked for the office renovation project scheduled to begin in July.
